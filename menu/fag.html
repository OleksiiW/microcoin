Micro Coin FAQ

What documents do I need to get a microloan?

You only need a passport to get a microloan.

How can I register in the system?

You can register in the system by visiting C:/microcoin/login.html or in the 'Cabinet' section in the menu.

What are the data requirements for registration?

The data requirements will be specified during the registration process.

How is my creditworthiness determined?

Your creditworthiness is based on data from the Central Bank, which in turn looks at your income level and credit history.

How can I get a loan?

To get a loan, you need to register in the system and click on the big red button in the main menu.

What is the maximum amount of loan that I can get?

The maximum amount is 517 000 hrn, but this can change and is not fixed.

What is the minimum amount of loan that I can get?

The minimum amount is 100 hrn now, but this can change and is not fixed.

What is the repayment term?

The maximum repayment term is one year, with interest accrued monthly.

What are the interest rates applied to loans?

The interest rate is 30% per month.

How can I repay the loan?

You can make partial payments or pay the entire amount at once. Payment details can be found in the appropriate menu item.

Can I repay the loan early?

Yes, you can.

Will there be a penalty for early repayment of the loan?

If the loan month has started, it is fully charged.

Are there any hidden fees or commissions in the system?

We cover all commission fees.

What happens if I can't repay the loan on time?

We will call you and try to resolve this issue with you. Unwillingness to cooperate may lead to court proceedings.

How can I change my registration data?

Yes, you can change your registration data, but you need to contact us at admin.micro.coin@gmail.com.

What can I do if I have problems with receiving or repaying the loan?

You can reach us at +380567434856 or admin.micro.coin@gmail.com.

What guarantees are there for the security of my data?

Your data is securely encrypted. In accordance with Ukrainian laws, we provide data on the microloan to the Central Bank.

Can I open multiple loans at the same time?

Yes, you can.

Can I register if I'm not a citizen of Ukraine?

Yes, you can.

Do you have a mobile application for more convenient lending?

At the moment, it is in development.
